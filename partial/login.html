About Whirlpool

Whirlpool is a platform that connects students of the University of Washington without vehicles to student drivers who want to find other students to carpool with. After creating an account, students provide contact information and customize their schedules. This allows students who drive to filter through students looking for a carpool and find their best matches based on personal preferences such as where students live and their quarterly schedules.

In addition, Whirlpool allows students the opportunity to network with each other and meet new people. It also has the potential to cut down carbon emissions by encouraging carpooling rather than using a taxi service or personal individual transportation. As an added bonus, students parking on campus who show Husky Cards receive a discount on parking!

Whirlpool was designed as a final project for INFO 343: Client-side Web Development.
